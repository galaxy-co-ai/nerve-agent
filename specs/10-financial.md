# Financial Nerve

## Overview

Turn tracked time into revenue automatically. See profitability per project, generate invoices from hours, and track payments—all without spreadsheets.

## Philosophy

- **Time is money, literally** — Auto-calculate revenue from tracked hours
- **Know your numbers** — Profitability at a glance, not after accounting
- **Invoice in seconds** — From tracked time to sent invoice
- **Cash flow visibility** — Know what's coming and what's overdue

## Integrations

### Time Tracking
- Revenue calculated from tracked hours
- Task-level granularity for invoices

### Sprint Stack
- Sprint completion triggers invoice reminder
- Budget tracking per sprint

### Projects
- Per-project rates and billing type
- Profitability tracked throughout project

### Stripe
- Payment processing
- Invoice hosting
- Payment status webhooks
